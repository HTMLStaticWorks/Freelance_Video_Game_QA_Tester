About QA Studio

One tester. One mission. Zero bugs shipped.

The Story

From competitive gamer to professional QA architect.

I started as a competitive esports player, obsessing over frame data, hitbox precision, and netcode consistency. That obsession evolved into a career in software QA, and eventually into QA Studio — a freelance practice built exclusively for indie game developers who need AAA-grade testing without AAA budgets.

Over 5 years, I've tested 86+ titles across 14 platforms, filed 2,400+ bugs, and helped studios pass console certification on their first submission. I don't just play games — I break them systematically so your players never have to.
